Our Process

From Vision to Bottle

Every Lopez fragrance is a journey of artistry, science, and passion.

01

Inspiration

We draw inspiration from emotions, memories, and the stories of empowered individuals. Each scent begins with a feeling we want to capture.

02

Selection

Our master perfumers carefully select premium ingredients from trusted sources worldwide, ensuring only the finest quality.

03

Creation

Through meticulous blending and testing, we craft each layer of the fragrance, balancing top, heart, and base notes to perfection.

04

Maturation

Each fragrance rests and matures, allowing the notes to harmonize and develop their full character over time.

05

Bottling

Finally, each perfume is hand-bottled in our signature vessels, ready to empower its owner with confidence and elegance.

Questions

Frequently Asked

How long do Lopez fragrances last?

Our Eau de Parfum formulations are designed to last 6-8 hours on the skin, with the base notes lingering even longer. Longevity can vary based on skin type, climate, and application method.

Are Lopez fragrances suitable for sensitive skin?

Yes! We use high-quality, hypoallergenic ingredients and conduct thorough testing. However, we always recommend doing a patch test if you have particularly sensitive skin.

Where can I purchase Lopez Fragrance products?

You can order directly through our social media pages or contact us via the form below. We offer delivery within Metro Manila and ship nationwide.

Do you offer samples?

Yes! We offer sample sets so you can experience our fragrances before committing to a full bottle. Contact us for more information on sample availability.

What's your return policy?

We want you to love your fragrance! If you're not completely satisfied, you can return unopened bottles within 7 days of delivery for a full refund.

Are your fragrances vegan and cruelty-free?

Absolutely! All Lopez Fragrance products are 100% cruelty-free and most of our scents are vegan-friendly. We're committed to ethical beauty practices.
